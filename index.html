OVERVIEW

As a requirement for an IT degree in Malayan Colleges Laguna, we have to undergo an IT Practicum that requires 486 credited hours to complete in the span of 12 weeks. We were given multiple options on how to conduct the IT Practicum and I chose the Accenture Technology Academy program which grants access to the AWS Educate platform where I would need to complete four specific modules that would generate 120 credited hours.

Due to the large amount of hours needed to complete the IT Practicum, I have to take courses from another platform alongside the ATA program where I would get the majority of my credited hours. As per recommendation, I enrolled in LinkedIn Learning.

AWS Educate

On this platform, I had to complete four courses that focused on Amazon Web Services which gave an overview on the capabilities of the service as well as instructions on the basics of operating and navigating the system, later courses also explained the different technologies involved in the service.

Introduction to the AWS Management Console

1 hour

A course that offers a brief overview on the significance of the AWS Management Console and how to navigate the AWS ecosystem using it.

Introduction to Cloud 101 (Labs)

8 hours

A course that introduces the core services of Amazon Web Services which include AWS S3, EC2, DynamoDB, RDS, VPC, and IAM. The course consisted of mostly hands-on activities that require the student to follow step-by-step instructions on how to operate the core services.

Cloud Support Associate

40 hours

A course that introduces skills needed to be a Cloud Support Engineer. The course covers multiple areas such as platform, network, software development, and security. It does not include any hands-on training and majority of the material is linked from third party sources.

Application Developer

50 hours

A course that introduces skills needed to be an Application Developer. The course covers multiple areas of software development such as architecture, DevOps, programming, scripting, web, mobile and gaming, it also covers databases and platforms. It does not include any hands-on training and majority of the material is linked from third party sources.

Design Thinking

This is the first required learning module which focuses on the concept of Design Thinking and how it relates to professionals.

Learning Design Thinking: Lead Change in Your Organization

2 hours 8 minutes

A course that introduces the idea of Design Thinking and how it can transform the way we do things when it comes to creating and delivering a product to the client.

Develop Your Creative Thinking and Innovation Skills

8 hours

A course that touches upon how creativity can affect our actions and approach to things, its importance is also emphasized when talking about the process of innovation.

Develop Critical-Thinking, Decision-Making, and Problem-Solving Skills

5 hours

A course that dicusses the significance of critical thinking when one is faced with different scenarios and how important it is when making decisions.

Project Management

This is the second required learning module which is about project management and how to optimize involved processes in order to improve productivity.

Project Management Foundations

3 hours 20 minutes

A course that discusses the ways you can ensure a project successfully accomplishes its goal using best practices as well as appropriate methodologies.

Become an Agile Project Manager

12 hours

A course that introduces us to the agile mindset and explains how to integrate it into our workflow using tools such as Microsoft Project.

Workplace Management

This is the third required learning module which is about workplace management and ways to improve it by developing a good work culture using better practices.

Develop Your Communication Skills and Interpersonal Influence

8 hours

A course that discusses the different types of communication and how excellent communication is essential to achieving good work culture.

5S Workplace Productivity

1 hour 36 minutes

A course that teaches the concept of "5S" which stands for "Sort, Straighten, Sweep, Standardize, and Self-Discipline". This concept helps to streamline the different areas of a business when applied properly.

Time Management Fundamentals

2 hours 53 minutes

A course that discusses the importance of having an organized work flow and time schedule in increasing one's productivity and efficiency.

Time Management: Working from Home

1 hour 25 minutes

A course that has same goal as "Time Management Fundamentals" but it is more focused on optimizing one's workflow and time schedule when working from home.

Business Etiquette: Phone, Email, and Text

58 minutes

A course that teaches the proper ways of sending an email, text message, and phone call on a professional basis.

Software Development

This is the first specialization module that I have chosen. I decided to focus on courses that involve web development since I am interested in that field and I think it would help me in the future.

Introduction to Web APIs

1 hour 1 minute

A course that gives a brief overview of what web APIs are and how they work, also their importance and widespread use in modern web development.

Become a Full-Stack Web Developer

29 hours

A course that covers multiple topics that are essential in web development. For front-end, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript were introduced followed by a basic explanation of the React.js framework. Git was included as the version control system. For the back-end, Node.js was chosen along with lessons about SQL and NoSQL. The course also included a brief lesson on DevOps foundations.

Become a RESTful API Developer

6 hours 17 minutes

A course that explains what APIs and RESTful APIs are, including a demonstration on how to set up a basic example as well as how to put security on it.

Become a MERN Stack JavaScript Developer

27 hours

A course that explains the MERN stack. MERN is an abbreviation for the technologies used in the stack and it includes MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js. As such, introductions and additional information are given to these technologies throughout the course.

Bootstrap 5 Essential Training

4 hours 20 minutes

A course that introduces Bootstrap 5 and the benefits of using it over regular CSS. It also teaches the fundamentals of using it.

Developing for Web Performance

2 hours 25 minutes

A course that focuses on the performance of web applications, the reasons why their slow and how to optimize them for better performance.

User Experience

Due to the large amount of hours needed to complete the IT Practicum, I have to take courses from another platform alongside the ATA program where I would get the majority of my credited hours. As per recommendation, I enrolled in LinkedIn Learning.

Become a User Experience Designer

8 hours 30 minutes

A course that explains the concept of user experience, the different aspects of it, and ways to put consumers first during the design process.
